Model Details

Pleated Shade Contemporary Lamp

Add a touch of modern elegance to your space with this distinctive two-part lamp featuring a beautifully pleated shade. The sculptural design combines a smooth, rounded base with an artfully pleated upper section that creates fascinating light patterns when illuminated.

  • Print in your preferred color to match your decor

  • Compatible with standard E27 bulb fittings with a 40mm hole for the fitting

  • Easy assembly - push the E27 fitting through the base, press the shade over the top, then use the fitting's screw mount to clamp both parts together

  • Uses the E27 lamp mount linked in the description below

  • Unique pleated shade creates beautiful light effects

  • Sturdy rounded base provides excellent stability

This decorative lamp brings contemporary style to any room with its thoughtful design. The pleated shade diffuses light in captivating patterns while the clean lines of the rounded base create a perfect contrast. The included link provides the exact E27 mount I used for this design, ensuring proper fit and assembly. Ideal for bedside tables, living rooms, or office spaces where you want to add both functional lighting and a design statement.
