Book Nook House - Narrow Townhouse Design

A charming book nook that transforms your bookshelf into a miniature townhouse scene. This clever design features a narrow facade with an arched window above and classic paneled door below, creating a cozy alleyway effect between your books. When lit from behind with the Bambu wireless RGB light, it casts a warm glow through the windows for a magical atmosphere.

All parts print separately for easy multi-color printing without an AMS - simply use a few small dabs of superglue for assembly. This light is compatible with the Bambu lighting kit linked below.

  • Print in your choice of colors to match your decor

  • Designed for Bambu wireless RGB light (linked in BOM)

  • Parts print separately - no AMS needed for multi-color

  • Simple assembly with small dabs of superglue

  • Fits standard bookshelves between books

  • Creates charming townhouse alley illusion

  • Warm glow effect when lit from behind

  • Perfect for adding whimsy to your reading nook
