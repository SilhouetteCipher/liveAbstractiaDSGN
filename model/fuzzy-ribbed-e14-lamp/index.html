E14 Sculptural Vase Lamp with Fuzzy Skin Effect

Transform your space with this elegant three-part lamp featuring a distinctive curved silhouette and captivating light diffusion. When illuminated, the textured exterior creates a mesmerizing sparkling effect that adds depth and ambiance to any room.

  • E14 compatible - use LED bulbs only due to heat

  • Three-part design: base mount, inner diffuser, and outer shade

  • Transparent PETG recommended for outer shade to maximize sparkling effect

  • Light PLA or transparent PETG recommended for inner diffuser

  • Utilizes fuzzy skin mode in Bambu Studio for unique textured finish

  • Print in your preferred color to complement your decor

This sophisticated lamp combines artistry with functionality. The curved profile and ribbed texture create beautiful light patterns when illuminated, while presenting as a sculptural piece even when off. The outer shade is provided as a separate file due to its specialized print settings, but all components are designed to work together perfectly.

Assembly Instructions:

1. Print the base and inner diffuser using standard settings
2. Print the outer shade using spiral vase mode with fuzzy skin enabled - IMPORTANT: The outer shade MUST be printed in spiral vase mode as that is how it's modelled
3. Insert the E14 mount into the base
4. Slide the inner diffuser into the base, ensuring the cable cutout is positioned at the bottom
5. Slip the outer shade over the assembled base, aligning with the three alignment pins on the base

Note: E14 fitting will be linked separately in the BOM section.base
