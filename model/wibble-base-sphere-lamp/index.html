Modern Fluted E27 Desk Lamp with Angled Base

A sleek desk lamp featuring clean lines and contemporary styling that wouldn't look out of place in a modern furniture catalog. The distinctive fluted shade creates interesting light patterns while the angled ribbed base adds visual texture and stability. The minimalist design works well in any workspace or bedside setting.

This lamp is compatible with Bambu lighting kits linked in the BOM below, making it easy to get up and running with proper wiring and components.

Key Features:

  • Print in your choice of filament and finish

  • Modern minimalist aesthetic with textured details

  • Angled base provides stable platform

  • Fluted shade creates attractive light diffusion

  • Compatible with standard E27 bulbs

  • Easy assembly with linked lighting components

  • Perfect for desk, bedside, or accent lighting
