Model Details

Wave Laptop Wall Mount

A sleek, minimalist wall mount for your laptop that combines form and function. This wave-designed holder keeps your workspace clean while adding a modern accent to your setup. The clever dovetail mounting system makes installation a breeze.

Perfect for home offices, dorm rooms, or anywhere you need to save desk space without sacrificing style.

Features:

  • Print in your preferred size and color to match your space

  • Available in multiple sizes to accommodate different laptop thicknesses

  • Includes dovetail joint mounting system - simply screw the dovetail to wall and slide mount into place

  • Ribbed design provides excellent airflow for laptop cooling

  • Lightweight yet sturdy construction

  • Low profile, minimalist aesthetic that complements modern interiors

For lighter laptops, adhesive mounting is possible as an alternative to screws. Protective foam pads (linked in BOM below) are recommended to prevent any scratches to your laptop.

Installation is quick and straightforward - no complicated hardware required!
