Ripple Vase Collection

Add a touch of modern elegance to your home with these striking ripple vases. The flowing, organic lines create a mesmerizing visual effect that catches light beautifully. Each vase features a distinctive textured pattern with gentle curves that give the illusion of movement, making them sculptural pieces of art even when not holding flowers.

  • Print in your preferred size and color to match your decor

  • No supports needed - designed for optimal 3D printing

  • Beautiful display piece on its own or as a flower vase

  • Available in both solid and spiral print patterns

  • Stunning textured surface catches and plays with light

The unique ripple texture provides an eye-catching contemporary aesthetic that works perfectly in minimalist spaces. These vases make excellent gifts and conversation pieces, combining form and function in one elegant design.
