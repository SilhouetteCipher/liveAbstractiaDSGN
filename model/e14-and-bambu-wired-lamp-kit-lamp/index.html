Lamp Bambu Wired Kit

e14
AND BAMBU WIRED LAMP KIT LAMP

Model Details

This elegant pleated lamp features a tall, faceted diffuser paired with a sturdy base, offering a refined blend of modern and classic aesthetics. The two-part design allows for versatile lighting setups—it’s fully compatible with both the Bambu LED Wired Light Kit and standard E14 light bulbs, making it adaptable for various lighting needs and preferences.

The clean geometry and vertical pleats create soft, directional glow, ideal for bedside tables, entryways, or ambient mood lighting in living areas.

  • Two-part lamp design: base and pleated diffuser


  • Compatible with both Bambu LED Wired Light Kit and E14 bulbs


  • Tool-free, easy assembly with a snug fit


  • Diffuser shape disperses light softly and evenly


  • Suitable for stylish home lighting or display accents
