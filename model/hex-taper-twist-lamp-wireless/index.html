TAPER TWIST LAMP WIRELESS

Model Details

This elegant, vase-mode lamp shade is designed to sit perfectly over the Bambu Wireless RGB Puck Light, transforming it into a striking centerpiece. Its sculptural hourglass form and flowing vertical contours provide a subtle play of light and shadow, adding a modern accent to any space. The single-wall spiral vase print ensures fast, material-efficient printing with a beautiful, seamless finish.

---

Key Features:

  • Designed specifically for the Bambu Wireless RGB Puck Light


  • Prints in spiral vase mode for efficient, single-wall construction


  • Organic hourglass silhouette creates dynamic light diffusion


  • Ideal for ambient or decorative lighting setups


  • Lightweight and easy to scale for different applications
