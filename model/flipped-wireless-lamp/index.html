Description

Fluted Barrel RGB Light Shade

Transform your space with this elegant fluted barrel light shade designed specifically for the Bambu wireless RGB puck. The gentle curved profile and precise vertical ribbing create stunning light diffusion effects that enhance any room's ambiance.

This modern lamp shade features a sophisticated barrel silhouette with delicate fluting that creates beautiful light and shadow patterns when illuminated. The thoughtfully designed structure perfectly diffuses the RGB lighting from your Bambu puck, transforming harsh direct light into a soft, ambient glow that can shift through a spectrum of colors.

Printed in spiral vase mode for rapid production and optimal light transmission, this shade is both practical and visually striking. The quick print time means you can easily create multiple shades to place throughout your home for consistent lighting design.

Features:

  • Designed specifically to fit perfectly over the Bambu wireless RGB puck

  • Transparent or light colored PLA recommended for best light diffusion (avoid matte filaments)

  • Print in your preferred color to complement your decor

  • Quick spiral vase mode print for rapid production

  • The light is compatible with the Bambu lighting kits linked below

  • Fluted texture creates beautiful light patterns and visual interest

  • Modern, sophisticated design enhances any room's aesthetic

  • Transforms your basic RGB puck into an elegant ambient light fixture

Add a touch of designer lighting to your space without the designer price tag. This versatile light shade works brilliantly in bedrooms, living areas, or as subtle accent lighting in any room.
