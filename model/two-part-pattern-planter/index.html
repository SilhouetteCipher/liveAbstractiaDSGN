Woven Pattern Planter with Integrated Drip Tray

Elevate your indoor greenery with this stylish two-part planter featuring an elegant woven texture pattern. The thoughtful design includes a matching integrated drip tray that catches excess water, protecting your surfaces while adding a coordinated aesthetic touch. The textured diamond pattern wraps around the planter, creating visual interest and depth that complements your houseplants beautifully.

  • Print in your preferred size and color to match your home décor

  • Two-part design: main planter pot and integrated drip tray

  • Textured diamond pattern provides modern, sophisticated appeal

  • Drainage holes allow proper water flow to protect plant roots

  • Perfect for houseplants, succulents, herbs, or decorative arrangements

This practical yet decorative planter brings together form and function with its attractive woven pattern and smart water management system. The clean lines and contemporary design make it suitable for any interior style, from minimalist to bohemian. Your plants will thrive while adding a touch of natural beauty to your living space in this thoughtfully designed container.
