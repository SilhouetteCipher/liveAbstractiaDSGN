Airport Cab-Over Truck Toy

Expand your mini vehicle fleet with this charming cab-over truck toy inspired by utility vehicles found at airports and industrial sites. With its distinctive high cab design and compact profile, this truck perfectly complements the airport tug for imaginative play scenarios.

The simplified yet recognizable design captures the essential features of these workhorse vehicles while maintaining a kid-friendly shape that's fun to maneuver across any surface. Sturdy construction ensures this truck is ready for countless hours of play.

  • Easy to print and assemble - just 7 parts total

  • Print the body, two axles, and four wheels

  • Simple assembly - thread axles through body and push wheels on

  • Print in your preferred color combinations

  • Detailed grille and cab features

  • Sturdy construction ideal for little hands

  • No supports required for easy printing

  • Perfect companion to the airport tug toy

Add this cab-over truck to your collection and let imaginations take flight with a growing fleet of miniature vehicles!
