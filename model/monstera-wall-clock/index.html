Layered Monstera Wall Clock

Bring a touch of botanical elegance to your home with this contemporary monstera leaf wall clock. The striking two-tone design features the iconic split-leaf pattern with a dimensional effect that creates subtle shadows on your wall, adding depth and visual interest to any room.

This modern timepiece combines natural inspiration with clean aesthetics for a sophisticated accent piece that complements both minimalist and tropical decor styles. The contrasting colors highlight the distinctive leaf structure, making it both a functional timekeeper and an artistic wall sculpture.

Perfect for living rooms, home offices, kitchens, or anywhere you want to add a stylish botanical touch without overwhelming your space.

Features:

  • Compatible with Bambu clock kit (linked in BOM below)

  • Print in your preferred style to match your decor

  • Two-color design with pause in the profile for filament change

  • Easy wall mounting with included mounting hardware

  • Clean, contemporary aesthetic works with any interior style

  • Unique dimensional effect creates subtle shadows

  • Modern botanical design that's never over-the-top

  • Easy to read with clear contrast between elements

  • Makes an impressive statement piece for any room

    • Elevate your wall decor with this designer-inspired botanical timepiece that perfectly balances natural forms with modern simplicity.
