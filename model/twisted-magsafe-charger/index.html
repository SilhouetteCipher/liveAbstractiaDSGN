MAGSAFE CHARGER

Model Details

This sculptural MagSafe stand combines function with a playful, organic form—perfect for desks, nightstands, or anywhere you use StandBy mode. It securely holds your iPhone in both vertical and horizontal orientations, making it ideal for smart displays, alarm clocks, or video playback while charging.

The model includes two versions:

  • One for the original MagSafe puck with the plastic cable


  • One for the updated puck with the braided cable (with a 0.5mm diameter difference to ensure a snug fit)

    • Designed for quick prints and everyday use, this stand blends aesthetic flair with practical utility. I recommend the linked adhesive pads to lock the charger down to your surface

    ---

    Key Features

  • Holds iPhone vertically or horizontally for full StandBy support


  • Includes two versions for both MagSafe puck styles (braided and original)


  • Sculpted form adds personality to your workspace or nightstand


  • Compact and support-free for easy printing


  • Precise 0.5mm size adjustment for optimal fit with different puck models
