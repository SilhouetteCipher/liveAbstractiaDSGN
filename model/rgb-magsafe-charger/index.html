MagSafe Charger Stand with RGB Accent

A sleek desktop MagSafe charging stand that combines functionality with ambient lighting. The sculptural design features flowing curves and a dedicated slot for the Bambu wireless RGB puck, transforming your charging station into a stylish accent piece for your desk or bedside table.

This two-part design prints the main body and legs separately, with the legs creating a tight press fit into the body for a secure, seamless connection. The integrated cable management keeps your setup tidy while the rear RGB slot adds customizable lighting to match your mood or workspace aesthetic.

Compatible with both old and new MagSafe chargers - simply print the plate version that matches your device. Assembly is straightforward: thread the cable through the body, press-fit your MagSafe puck, then slide the RGB puck into the rear slot.

Print Notes: Ensure any supports are clear of the holes on the body to avoid interference with the tight press fit assembly.

Key Features:

  • Two-part tight press-fit design for easy printing and secure assembly

  • Compatible with old and new MagSafe chargers

  • Integrated cable management channel

  • RGB accent lighting with Bambu wireless puck

  • Must be printed at original dimensions for proper fit

  • Clean, modern aesthetic for any workspace
