Model Details

Ripple Toothbrush Holder

Modernize your bathroom with this minimalist toothbrush holder featuring elegant ripple textures. The sleek, cylindrical design combines form and function with subtle wave patterns that add visual interest to your countertop while keeping your toothbrushes organized and accessible.

This contemporary toothbrush holder is available in both spiral vase mode for a rapid but decorative print, or a solid print for enhanced functionality. For the spiral vase version, I recommend the adhesive pads linked in the BOM to provide additional stability if you find it too lightweight.

Features:

  • Print in your preferred size and color to match your bathroom aesthetic

  • Available in spiral vase mode (quick print, decorative) or solid print (functional, stable)

  • Contemporary ripple texture design for modern bathrooms

  • Easy to clean and maintain

  • Perfect for organizing toothbrushes and keeping them upright

Give your bathroom organization a stylish upgrade with this practical yet sophisticated toothbrush holder that complements any modern bathroom decor.
