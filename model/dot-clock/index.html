Desktop Dot Clock - Multi-Layer Color Display

This striking desktop clock features a clever two-part design with an outer dotted frame and inner mechanism holder that creates beautiful multi-layered visual effects. The perforated pattern allows the inner component's finish to show through, creating stunning depth and contrast when you mix materials or finishes.

The compact square format fits perfectly on desks, nightstands, or shelves while the press-fit assembly makes it simple to customize and build. Choose contrasting materials for bold statements or subtle tonal combinations for understated elegance - the dotted overlay effect adds visual interest from any angle.

This clock is compatible with Bambu clock kits linked in the BOM below.

  • Print in your choice of materials and finishes

  • Two-part design creates multi-layered color effects

  • Designed to specific dimensions for proper fit

  • Press-fit assembly - no tools required

  • Compact desktop size perfect for any space

  • Dotted pattern creates striking visual depth
