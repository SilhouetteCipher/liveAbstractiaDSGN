Modern Abstract Wibble Vase

Add a striking sculptural element to your space with this modern abstract vase that transforms from every viewing angle. The fluid, twisted form creates an ever-changing silhouette that catches the eye and sparks conversation. Its contemporary design works equally well as a functional vase or standalone art piece.

This model can be printed in spiral vase mode for a quick, decorative-only version that showcases the flowing lines beautifully. For a more functional piece that can hold water and flowers, choose the solid print option. If you opt for the lightweight spiral vase version and find it needs more stability, I recommend using the adhesive pads linked in the BOM to secure it in place.

  • Print in any color to match your decor

  • Scale to your preferred size

  • Choose between spiral vase mode (decorative) or solid print (functional)

  • Unique twisted design looks different from every angle

  • Works as both vase and sculptural art piece

  • Modern aesthetic suits contemporary interiors
