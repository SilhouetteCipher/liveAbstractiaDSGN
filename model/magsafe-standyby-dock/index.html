MagSafe iPhone Standby Mode Mount with Adjustable Positioning

A practical desktop mount designed specifically for iPhone standby mode that keeps your device at the perfect viewing angle. The ribbed design adds grip and style while the adjustable positioning lets you switch between horizontal and vertical orientations. Clean lines and a stable base make this an ideal bedside or desk companion.

Available in two versions to match your MagSafe charger - one for the original MagSafe and another for the newer braided cable version that's slightly more compact. I recommend using the adhesive pads linked in the BOM to keep the mount securely in place and prevent it from sliding around on smooth surfaces.

Key Features:

  • Print in your choice of filament and finish

  • Scale to your preferred size for different setups

  • Holds iPhone in both horizontal and vertical positions

  • Perfect for standby mode viewing

  • Two versions available - original and braided cable MagSafe

  • Ribbed texture provides secure grip and visual appeal

  • Stable base with recommended adhesive pads for extra grip

  • Ideal for bedside tables or desks
