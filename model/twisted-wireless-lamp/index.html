Model Details

Twisted Ripple Light

Bring elegant ambiance to your space with this sleek twisted design that creates stunning light patterns. The flowing vertical ripples catch and disperse light beautifully, making this piece a striking focal point whether lit or unlit.

This light prints rapidly in spiral vase mode and is compatible with the Bambu lighting kits linked below. The dynamic twisted form creates mesmerizing shadow patterns that transform any room into a sophisticated space.

Features:

  • Print in your own choice of material and color to match your decor

  • Available in spiral vase mode for rapid decorative printing or solid mode for durability

  • Compatible with Bambu lighting RGB puck for customizable lighting effects

  • Clean, modern design complements contemporary and minimalist interiors

  • Easy assembly with no supports needed

The light is available in two versions - a quick spiral vase mode print that's perfect for decorative use, or a more solid functional print. I recommend the adhesive pads linked in the BOM to secure the spiral vase version if you find it too lightweight.

Print at your desired size to create anything from a subtle accent light to a dramatic statement piece.
