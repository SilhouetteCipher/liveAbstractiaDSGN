Model Details

Wave Tealight Holders Collection

Add a touch of modern elegance to your space with these sculptural wave tealight holders. The undulating pattern creates a mesmerizing play of light and shadow when illuminated, transforming any room with a warm, inviting ambiance.

This two-part design features a textured upper section that diffuses light beautifully and a solid base that provides stability. The clean, contemporary aesthetic makes these tealight holders perfect for any interior style, from minimalist to eclectic.

Available as a set or individually, these versatile pieces work beautifully as standalone decorative items or grouped together for a more dramatic display.

Features:

  • Compatible with standard 38mm LED tealights

  • Two-part design - top and bottom sections slot together easily

  • Print in your preferred style to match your decor

  • Optimized for printing with transparent or light-colored filament for top section

  • Creates beautiful light patterns when illuminated

  • Modern, sculptural design adds interest even when not lit

  • Stackable design allows for creative display options

  • Quick and easy print with no supports needed

  • Clean, contemporary aesthetic works with any interior style

    • Transform your space with these elegant, functional tealight holders that blend artistic design with practical lighting solutions.

    BOM:

  • 38mm LED Tealights
