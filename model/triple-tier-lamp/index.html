Lamp Bambu Wired Kit

Triple
TIER LAMP

Model Details

This elegant 3-tier lamp design features a clean, fluted texture and modern silhouette, making it an eye-catching accent piece for any space. Designed to print in three separate parts, it offers an easy and engaging way to mix and match colors—even with a single-extruder printer. Whether used as a cohesive unit or with contrasting sections, this lamp brings a refined and customizable aesthetic to your setup.

It is fully compatible with both the Bambu Wireless RGB Puck Lights and the Bambu LED Wired Light Kit, giving you the flexibility to choose your preferred lighting option. Links to these lights are included in the bill of materials at the bottom of this listing, and purchasing through those links helps support the creator.

Key Features

  • Modular 3-part design for easy printing and creative color mixing


  • Clean vertical fluting adds texture and visual interest


  • Compatible with Bambu Wireless RGB Puck and Wired LED Light Kit
