Model Details

Geometric Two-Part Table Lamp with Textured Shade

A striking two-part lamp design featuring a bold geometric pattern that creates captivating light and shadow effects. The textured cylindrical shade sits perfectly on a sleek base, offering both ambient lighting and visual interest. The geometric faceted surface adds depth and character to any space.

This lamp is compatible with Bambu lighting kits linked in the BOM below and also accepts standard E14 bulb mounts for flexible lighting options.

Key Features:

  • Print in your preferred filament and finish

  • Two-part design for easy printing and assembly

  • Geometric textured shade creates dynamic lighting effects

  • Compatible with both Bambu LED kits and E14 bulb mounts

  • Stable base with clean modern lines

  • Eye-catching pattern adds visual interest to any room

  • Perfect for accent or mood lighting
