Description


Ripple LED Table Lamp

Transform your space with this elegant ripple-effect table lamp that brings a warm, inviting glow to any room. The distinctive curved silhouette and textured vertical pattern create stunning light diffusion effects, casting gentle shadows while providing ambient illumination.

This modern lamp features an innovative three-part design: a sturdy base, a textured outer shade with fuzzy skin finish, and an inner diffuser that softens the light for a cozy atmosphere. The sculptural form makes it an artistic statement piece even when not illuminated.

Perfect for bedside tables, desks, shelves, or accent tables where you want to add both style and functional lighting.

Features:

  • Compatible with Bambu lighting kits linked in BOM below

  • Print in your preferred style to match your decor

  • Three-part design allows for creative color combinations

  • Textured outer shade with fuzzy skin for visual interest

  • Inner diffuser ensures perfect light dispersion

  • Designed for E14 fittings and LED candle-style bulbs

  • Modern, mid-century inspired design complements any interior

  • Compact footprint works well in small spaces

  • Creates a warm, inviting ambient glow

Elevate your home lighting with this designer-inspired lamp that combines form and function in a beautiful, printable package.
