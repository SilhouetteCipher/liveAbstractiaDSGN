WIBBLE LAMP E14

Model Details

Wavy Column Ambient Light

Add a touch of modern elegance to any space with this wavy column light that creates beautiful ambient illumination. The undulating vertical ridges diffuse light in captivating patterns, making it a stunning accent piece whether lit or unlit.

This two-part lamp is designed for E14 fittings and looks fantastic with LED bulbs (linked in BOM below).

Key Features:

  • Print in your own size and color to complement any interior style

  • Compatible with standard E14 light fittings

  • Two-part design with separate base and shade for easy assembly

  • Undulating texture creates mesmerizing light patterns

  • Modern organic aesthetic that works in contemporary or minimalist spaces

The gentle wave pattern gives this lamp a dynamic quality that brings visual interest to any room. When illuminated, the textured surface creates a play of light and shadow that adds warmth and dimension to your space.

Simple to print and assemble, this versatile light fixture makes a sophisticated addition to bedside tables, shelves, or as accent lighting in living areas. LED bulbs are recommended for energy efficiency and reduced heat output.
