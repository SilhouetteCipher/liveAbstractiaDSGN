Two-Part Collared Vase - Flowing Exterior Design

This sophisticated two-part vase features a striking flowing exterior collar that separates from an inner vessel, creating endless possibilities for material and finish combinations. The elegant sculptural form adds contemporary style to any space while the practical dual-component design makes it both functional and visually stunning.

The flowing exterior creates beautiful shadow play and visual interest, while the removable inner vase makes cleaning and water changes effortless. Mix and match materials to create custom looks that perfectly complement your decor, from subtle tonal combinations to bold contrasting statements.

  • Print in your choice of materials and finishes

  • Scale to suit your space and flower arrangements

  • Two-part design enables stunning material combinations

  • Flowing exterior creates sculptural appeal

  • Easy cleaning with removable inner vessel

  • Functional solid print design
