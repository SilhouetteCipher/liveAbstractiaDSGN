Ribbed Sphere MagSafe Phone Stand

Enhance your charging experience with this stylish ribbed sphere MagSafe phone stand. Designed with both functionality and aesthetics in mind, this stand securely holds your iPhone in either vertical or horizontal orientation while charging, perfect for video calls, watching content, or just keeping your device visible while it powers up.

Available in two versions to accommodate both classic MagSafe chargers and the newer braided MagSafe cables, ensuring compatibility with your existing charging equipment. The distinctive ribbed texture adds a contemporary design element to any desk, nightstand, or workspace.

Features:

  • Print in your preferred color to match your space

  • Dual orientation support - works in both portrait and landscape modes

  • Available in two versions for compatibility with original or braided MagSafe chargers

  • Modern ribbed sphere design adds visual interest to your space

  • Stable base keeps your device secure while charging

  • Precision-designed cavity ensures perfect fit for MagSafe chargers

Upgrade your charging setup with this practical and stylish MagSafe stand that combines modern design with everyday functionality.
