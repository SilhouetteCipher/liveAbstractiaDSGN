Ribbed MagSafe Stand - Vertical/Horizontal

A stylish MagSafe charging stand featuring elegant ribbed detailing that matches the modern aesthetic. The oval design supports both vertical and horizontal phone positioning, perfect for StandBy mode. Simply snap your iPhone onto the integrated MagSafe charger for a secure connection while keeping your device visible and accessible.

Two versions available to accommodate different MagSafe charger models - the newer version is designed for the slightly smaller charger with braided cable.

I recommend using the adhesive pad linked in the BOM to secure the dock to your chosen surface.

  • Print in your choice of color to complement your space

  • Supports both vertical and horizontal phone orientation

  • Perfect for StandBy mode on your nightstand or desk

  • Two versions for different MagSafe charger sizes

  • Newer version fits smaller MagSafe with braided cable

  • Sophisticated ribbed texture matches your decor collection

  • Use adhesive pad (linked in BOM) to lock dock in place

  • Cable management channel keeps things tidy
