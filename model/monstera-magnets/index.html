Monstera Magnets for Refrigerator or Metal Surfaces

Bring a touch of tropical flair to your space with these stylish monstera leaf magnets. Each beautifully detailed leaf captures the iconic split-leaf pattern that makes monstera plants so distinctive. Perfect for securing notes, photos, or simply adding a botanical accent to any magnetic surface.

The design features a clean, minimalist approach that highlights the elegant leaf structure while maintaining a contemporary aesthetic. Each magnet provides strong holding power using the Bambu 6mm x 2mm magnets (linked in BOM below).

Two printing options available:

  • Single color profile for a quick, clean print

  • Two-tone profile with pause for filament change to create contrasting vein details

    • Features:

  • Print in your preferred size and color to match your decor

  • Compatible with Bambu 6mm x 2mm magnets (linked below)

  • Strong magnetic hold for practical everyday use

  • Modern botanical design works with any interior style

  • Easy to print with optimized profiles included

  • Two-tone option for added visual interest

  • Clean, minimalist design that won't overwhelm your space

  • The magnets will pressfit. It may need a littel force as the press fit has to be tight. If you would prefer to use glue just scale the magnets up a tiny amount and then the hole will be oversized and you can use superglue

    • Upgrade your refrigerator, whiteboard, or any magnetic surface with these eye-catching monstera magnets that blend natural inspiration with practical function.
