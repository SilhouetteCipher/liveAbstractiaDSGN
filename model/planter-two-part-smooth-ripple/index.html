Model Details

Minimalist Ripple Planter with Hidden Drainage

Enhance your indoor greenery with this sleek planter featuring a subtle wave ripple texture that adds a refined touch to any space. The clean cylindrical design balances contemporary minimalism with just enough texture to create visual interest while complementing your plants rather than competing with them.

  • Two-part design with hidden integrated drainage system

  • Subtle exterior ripple texture creates a refined tactile experience

  • Clean, modern silhouette suits any décor style

  • Print in your preferred color to match your space

  • Can be scaled to fit various plant sizes

  • Perfect for displaying houseplants, herbs, or succulents

This thoughtfully designed planter combines practicality with understated elegance. The two-part construction allows excess water to drain away from roots while maintaining a clean look with no visible drainage holes. The gentle rippled exterior catches light in a sophisticated way, adding depth and interest to this contemporary container.
