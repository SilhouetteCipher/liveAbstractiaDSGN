Ribbed Teardrop Wireless RGB Puck Light

A sleek teardrop-shaped lamp with distinctive ribbed detailing that creates beautiful light patterns when illuminated. The elegant curves and textured surface make this a standout piece for any modern interior. The clean lines and sophisticated form work perfectly as accent lighting or ambient mood lighting.

This lamp is designed to house wireless RGB puck lights, giving you full control over the lighting atmosphere. The ribbed texture casts interesting shadows and highlights, adding depth and visual interest to your space. Print it in any filament that suits your decor - from crisp whites to bold accent colors.

This lamp is compatible with the Bambu lighting kits linked below in the BOM.

Key Features:

  • Print in your own choice of material and finish

  • Compatible with wireless RGB puck lights

  • Ribbed texture creates beautiful light patterns

  • Modern teardrop silhouette

  • Perfect for accent or ambient lighting
